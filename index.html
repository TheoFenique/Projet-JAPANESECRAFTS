This project began from the vision of a chef who aspires to create the ultimate dining experience. That chefâ€™s name is Seita. He is the owner and chef of terra Tokyo Italian, a Michelin Star winning Italian restaurant in Singapore. Originally this project started with the goal of creating original dishware to be used at terra Tokyo Italian, but chef Seita saw a greater potential to share Arita Ware, the pride of Japan, with the rest of the world. Thus it was decided that, in addition to being used at terra Tokyo Italian, this series of original tableware would receive a limited release available only on Kickstarter.
